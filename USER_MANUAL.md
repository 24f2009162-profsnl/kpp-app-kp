# KPP User Manual 📖

Welcome to **Kaiitzn Pocket Pay (KPP)**! This guide will help you navigate the platform and make the most of your campus economy.

## 1. Getting Started
- **Sign In**: Use your Student ID (e.g., ME21B001) or sign in with your Google account.
- **Dashboard**: View your XLM balance, KPP Points, and recent transactions at a glance.

## 2. Earning Points (Earn Section)
- **Marketplace**: Browse available "Gigs" posted by clubs or departments.
- **Apply**: Click "View Details" and then "Apply Now" to start a task.
- **Complete**: Once finished, mark the task as complete in the "My Tasks" tab to receive your KPP Points.
- **Refer & Earn**: Share your referral code with friends to earn 1500 KPP Points for each successful sign-up.

## 3. Redeeming Points
- **Redeem Tab**: Go to the "Redeem" tab in the Earn section.
- **Shop**: Browse college essentials like library fine waivers, canteen vouchers, and tech workshop passes.
- **Redeem**: Click "Redeem Now" to spend your points. You'll receive a confirmation email with your voucher.

## 4. Payments (Pay Section)
- **Scan to Pay**: Use the QR scanner to pay at campus canteens or stores.
- **Send XLM**: Transfer XLM directly to other students using their KPP ID or Wallet Address.

## 5. Community (Forum)
- **Discuss**: Join campus discussions about Stellar, micro-earning, or general student life.
- **Earn Badges**: Be active in the forum to earn exclusive badges and awards.

## 6. Profile & Security
- **Customization**: Change your avatar by selecting a preset or using your **Live Camera**.
- **Themes**: Toggle between Dark Mode and the **Neon Pink** theme in your profile settings.
- **Security**: Your account is protected by **StellarGuard**, providing real-time monitoring and multi-sig security.
- **Past Usernames**: View your history of usernames in the profile section.

## 7. Live Metrics
- **Analytics**: Track your success rate, global rank, and earnings trend in the Analytics tab of the Earn section.
